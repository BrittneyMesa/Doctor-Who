BOSS

BOSS from Biomorphic Organisational Systems Supervisor, is a supercomputer that appears in The Green Death (1973). It has a megalomaniacal personality, and has been programmed to be inefficient, so that it can make the same kind of intuitive leaps of logic as humans. It is able to brainwash humans, including Captain Mike Yates who later suffers a nervous breakdown as a result. It is responsible for producing the chemicals that mutate maggots into giant maggots. BOSS plans to interface with all computers on Earth and enslave humanity. Stevens, a human brainwashed by BOSS, sacrifices himself when his mental programming is partially broken by the Third Doctor, blowing himself and the computer up as the Doctor escapes.
